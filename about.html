About ModernCo.

We're a team of passionate designers, developers, and digital strategists dedicated to helping businesses succeed in the digital world.

Our Story

How we became a leading digital solutions provider

Founded in 2010, ModernCo. began with a simple mission: to help businesses leverage the power of digital technology to grow and succeed in an increasingly connected world.

What started as a small team of three passionate tech enthusiasts has grown into a full-service digital agency with over 50 talented professionals across design, development, marketing, and strategy.

Over the years, we've had the privilege of working with hundreds of clients across various industries, from startups to Fortune 500 companies, helping them transform their digital presence and achieve their business goals.
